Aesthetician Certification Course
Medical aesthetics is a lucrative business opportunities for aesthetics practitioners today. Learning how to effectively perform medical aesthetics procedures will enhance your practice, increase your income and provide widespread patient satisfaction. This 2 day Aesthetician Certification Course will equip you with information required to take your practice to next level.

Certification Goals are outlined below:
  • Fundamental basic science related to cosmetic medicine
  • Fundamental clinical cosmetic medicine covering major aesthetic applications including:
    • Aesthetic Consultation
    • IPL
    • Radiofrequency
    • Skin Cosmeceuticals
    • Chemical peeling
    • Microdermabrasion
    • Skin needling
    • Spa management
    • Practice development
